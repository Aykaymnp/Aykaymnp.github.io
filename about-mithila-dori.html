About Mithila Dori

Our Story

Mithila Dori was created with a passion for handmade jewellery and a love for traditional Indian fashion. Our brand focuses on designing imitation jewellery and hair accessories that combine elegance, affordability, and quality.

We believe jewellery is more than an accessory. It reflects personality and culture. That's why we carefully design every artificial jewellery piece to suit modern women while preserving traditional aesthetics.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide affordable fashion jewellery that looks premium without the high price tag. From bridal imitation jewellery to everyday ethnic jewellery, we ensure each design meets our quality standards.

Customers across India trust Mithila Dori for stylish jewellery available online through our website, Amazon, and Meesho platforms. We continue to grow while staying committed to authenticity and craftsmanship.

Why Choose Our Jewellery

At Mithila Dori, every piece of jewellery is crafted with attention to detail, ensuring durability and beauty at affordable prices. Our handmade jewellery blends traditional charm with modern fashion trends, making it perfect for any occasion.

Whether you are looking for ethnic jewellery for festivals, bridal imitation jewellery for weddings, or affordable fashion jewellery for daily wear, we have something special for you. Our artificial jewellery collection is carefully designed to enhance your style without compromising comfort.

We are proud to be an online jewellery store serving customers across India. You can shop directly through our retail store or find our exclusive collection on Amazon and Meesho. Our mission is to make high-quality imitation jewellery accessible to everyone.

Discover beautiful earrings, necklaces, bangles, and decorative hair accessories that complement both traditional and modern outfits. At Mithila Dori, fashion meets affordability.
